Account Help

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, select Forgot password and follow the instructions sent to your email address.

How do I change my email or password?

Open the app, go to Settings, and use the account update options available there.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings, and select Delete account. This action permanently removes your account and associated data.

Data and Privacy

If you delete your account, your account data and linked records are removed from the app.

For more information about how data is handled, please refer to your privacy policy page if you have one linked on your website or in App Store Connect.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure you are using the latest version of the app.
  • Check your internet connection if data is not loading correctly.
  • Try logging out and back in if your account details do not refresh.
  • For subscription issues, restore purchases from the app if available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I export my CPD records?

Yes. MyCPD allows you to export your records as PDF or CSV.

Can I share my CPD profile with employers?

Yes. You can create a live shareable view link and use filters to control what is shown.

Will new matching activities appear in a shared view automatically?

Yes. If you create a filtered shared link, future activities that match those filters will appear in that live shared view.

Report a Bug or Suggest a Feature

You can use the in-app feedback form in the Settings area to report bugs or suggest improvements.

If something is not working as expected, include as much detail as possible, such as what happened, what you expected, and which device you were using.
